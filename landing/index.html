Features

Everything you need, nothing you don't

𝕏

X & email login

Sign in with your X (Twitter) account via OAuth 2.0 PKCE, or receive a magic link by email. No passwords, ever.

🔐

PGP end-to-end encryption

Add your PGP public key in settings. Senders encrypt messages with your key — only you can decrypt them.

🔒

Encrypted files & voice

Every uploaded file and voice note is encrypted at rest with AES-256-GCM. Decrypt instantly in your browser via the secure viewer.

📧

Resend optional

Platform sends email notifications from yo@hollr.to by default. Plug in your own Resend key for custom delivery.

⏱️

Timed canvas

An always-running timer reminds senders how long they've been composing. Focus and intentionality built in.

🎤

Voice recording

Record a voice note directly in the browser. Encrypted and linked in your notification email.

📎

File attachments

Drag and drop any file. Encrypted, uploaded securely, and linked in the email — no limits on type.

🌐

10 languages

English, French, German, Italian, Spanish, Dutch, Chinese, Hindi, Japanese, Russian. More coming.

🌙

Dark & light mode

Follows your system preference. Manual toggle always available.

🛠️

Fully open source

MIT licensed. Fork it, self-host it, extend it. No vendor lock-in, ever.
